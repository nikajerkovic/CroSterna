CroSterna: Age estimation from sternal fusion and rib ossification in Croatian population

This app was developed on the modern Croatian population MSCT scans on an age and sex stratified sample of 180 individuals. It uses four variables: Xiphoid process and mesosternum fusion (FX), ossification of the second to seventh costal cartilages at the rib ends (OR), Ossification of the second to seventh costal cartilages at the sternal ends (OS), and sex of individual. The app provides lower and the upper predicted age of an individual with a cross-validation and test set accuracy of 92%.

Instructions: The traits should be scored according to the descriptions available in the study.
